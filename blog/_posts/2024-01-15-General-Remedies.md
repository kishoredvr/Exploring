This article summarizes general remedies intended to address potential indications of negative planetary influences according to the provided information. Consistency in practicing these remedies is emphasized for achieving better results. The remedies discussed are generally simple to follow and can be adopted by people of any religion.

**Sun (Surya)**

Indications: Problems with your father or higher authorities, confidence issues, heart-related problems, or spine problems. Addressing skin-related diseases by applying Sun remedies can also be effective.

**Remedies:**

Perform Surya Namaskar daily at sunrise.

Offer water to the sun in a copper vessel.

Consider wearing a ruby ring (with caution).

**Moon (Chandra)**

Indications: Problems with your mother, issues with food habits (e.g., not getting your favorite food), or emotional sensitivity due to negative planetary influences.

**Remedies:**

Donate rice monthly.

Wear silver ornaments.

Avoid food other than milk after sunset, or consume "pongali" (a sweet rice dish) as an offering.

**Mars (Kuja)**

Indications: Legal issues (court cases), disputes with others, debt problems, anger issues, or strained relationships with siblings.

**Remedies:**

Wear a red coral ring (with caution).

Worship goddesses with red flowers.

Control anger.

Engage in daily physical activities or games to release excess energy.

**Mercury (Budha)**

Indications: Communication issues, difficulty in decision-making, transmittable diseases, or challenges in business, calculations, or research.

**Remedies:**

Wear an emerald ring with gold (with caution).

Consume green leafy vegetables.

Feed green leaves to cows.

Provide food or support to orphaned children.

**Jupiter (Guru)**

Indications: Problems with higher education, lack of luck, difficulty finding guidance, or issues that go against tradition.

**Remedies:**

Wear a yellow sapphire ring with gold (with caution).

Donate to religious or educational causes.

Donate sweets on Thursdays (preferably as temple prasad).

Avoid speaking against religious activities.

**Venus (Shukra)**

Indications: Problems with marriage, relationships, financial issues related to luxury, lack of harmony, issues with appearance or maintaining luxurious items, or problems with reproductive health.

**Remedies:**

Regularly engage in arts like dance or music.

Consider wearing a diamond in a silver ring (with caution).

**Saturn (Shani)**

Indications: Work or job-related difficulties, delays in tasks, chronic health issues (especially bone or leg problems), long-lasting problems, or conflicts with subordinates.

**Remedies:**

Help the underprivileged through charitable activities.

Observe fasts on Saturdays.

Physically involve yourself in helping elderly people at old age homes or hospitals.

**Rahu**

Indications: Inability to identify truth, mistaking wrong things for right, involvement in addictions (drinking, smoking, drugs), misunderstandings, excessive materialism, or confusion.

**Remedies:**

Avoid fast food, follow a proper diet, and consume organic food.

Avoid bad habits like alcohol and cigarettes.

Donate sesame seeds.

Consume a basil leaf (tulsi) as the first thing in the morning daily.

**Ketu**

Indications: Anxiety, isolation, sudden unexpected changes (like problematic surgeries), or detachments from people.

**Remedies:**

Feed pets and animals without expecting anything in return.

Ensure pets are not tied up.

Consider wearing a cat's eye in a silver ring (with caution).

Regularly feed birds in a designated spot.

**Important Note on Rings**

Wearing rings should be a last resort and preferably done after consulting an astrologer
